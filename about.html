Our Story

Founded in the heart of Nairobi, Uncle SAM Bakery has been serving smiles since 2010. What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery known for its warmth, creativity, and irresistible treats.

Our mission is to bring joy through every bite!
